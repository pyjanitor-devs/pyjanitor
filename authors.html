Contributors¶

Once you have added your contribution to pyjanitor, please add your name using this markdown template:

`@githubname <https://github.com/githubname>`_ | `contributions <https://github.com/ericmjl/pyjanitor/issues?q=is%3Aclosed+mentions%3Agithubname>`_

You can copy/paste the template with backticks (not quotation marks).

Contributions that did not leave a commit trace are indicated in bullet points below each user’s username.
